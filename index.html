About Us

Platypus changes the way we learn and find a new job.

We believe that changing careers should not be as difficult, time consuming and expensive as it is today. Instead of going back to university to do a one to two year course that is beyong your means and that does not guarantee you the job you really want, we put you in touch with companies that are willing to hire you and give you a chance on the condition that you reskill before your first day at work.
