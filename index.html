About Us

Platypus changes the way we learn and find a new job.

We believe that changing careers should not be as difficult, time consuming and expensive as it is today. Instead of going back to university to do a one to two year course that is beyong your means and that does not guarantee you the job you really want, we put you in touch with companies that are willing to hire you and give you a chance on the condition that you reskill before your first day at work.

Services

Changing Career

We put you in touch with organisations that look for bright people with highly transferable skills to fuel diversity and innovation. Our goal is to help you find your dream job.

Reskill

Agree with your new employer on the course you need to complete before your first day at work. Targeted learning is far more efficient than starting from scratch.

Connect

We connect individuals that want to enter each other's respective industries. We believe that there is no better source of information than the people with real experience.
